Where I am now

Iwork on telepathy. That's an unusual sentence to put on a personal website, which is exactly the problem. Direct experience needs rigorous methodology to be more than story; rigorous methodology needs direct experience to be more than measurement. I co-founded The Telepathy Center to make this work a field, not a footnote.

Beyond TTC, I'm in the broader question of what comes next: what disclosure actually requires, what serious inquiry looks like at the edge of what science currently holds, what institutions don't yet exist but need to. I write, I talk, and I build what isn't there yet.
